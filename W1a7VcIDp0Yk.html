How to start investing (portfolio income for beginners)

Income comes easily to everyone. But, if you don't have a discretionary income, then this is it's time to look for it.In this article, we will talk about how to start investing your money so that it can "feed" you and your family. your loved ones.1) Keep track of expensesFirst, determine how much money you have to spend right now:What is your monthly income? What is the amount of your mortgage loan? How long have you been investing?Remember the saying: "an investment is like a thief. You catch him when he's stealing. You catch him stealing more often than you catch the thief."It's a good rule of thumb: 10-20% of each income you receive, at least 10-20% be spent on taking care of your lifestyle. (This is your usual healthy lifestyle, not your "consumer Welfare" that is constantly changing from job to job.)2) Think through a plan of actionIf you have a certain expenditure that you can afford, then feel free to adjust it.Consider:what is the maximum amount of your income that you can safely spend on this item now;what steps you need to take to increase your earnings in order to make this expenditure work for you;what additional sources of income you have;how you can earn extra money;what expenses you can opt out of (if you don't like them now, and you can afford them later).3) Optimize the growth of your moneyThis is probably the most important tip for creating passive income. money.As Richard Florida so aptly put it: "Money should work."What is very interesting about this is that in the financial sector, there are a lot of "stones" that you can use to start investing your money:stocks, bonds, real estate funds,banks,employers, etc.Where and how you invest your money — this is strictly your business!So why not start investing your money now in order to have it work for you?4) Earn money on your expensesWe have all heard the phrase, "if you don't have enough money, borrow it."But if you don't have time to pay off the loan, use the money from it to invest.In this age of ubiquitous Internet penetration, it has become extremely easy to get started with small incomes:You will need a small car, no. 1 a bicycle, no. 2 a set of basic tools and no. 3 — the so-called "financial cushion".In the age of ubiquitous Internet penetration, it has become extremely easy to borrow money on the Internet (and earn a small income from it).Start it up a piggy Bank and make good use of it there. Start it up a piggy Bank Express.5) Sell your " lost " moneyYou can't always keep money on a Bank Deposit.
